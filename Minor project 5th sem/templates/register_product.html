Register product & generate QR

Enter the key product details. 4REAL will store this record and create a unique QR identity that your customers can scan to verify authenticity.

After saving, you will return to the dashboard where this product appears in the list, with options to view, edit, delete and open the generated QR.

4REAL registration tips

Strong product metadata makes your fake‑product detection more reliable and easier to audit.

Product name
Use a clear name + variant (size, flavor, model) so support teams can identify items quickly.
Batch / lot
Batch numbers help you trace issues and detect if a specific lot is being faked in the market.
Unique code
Each product gets a unique QR code in the system. If someone copies that code, scans from a fake item will still be detected.
Once this form is submitted, 4REAL generates a QR that points to your verification URL (for example de>/verify/<product_code>). When a customer scans it, your Flask app checks this product entry and returns “Genuine” or “Fake / Not found”.
