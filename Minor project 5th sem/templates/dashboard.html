Activity & verification flow

A quick snapshot of how your fake product protection is performing.

Step 1 · Register
Admin adds product details and 4REAL generates a unique QR-code identity.
Step 2 · Print & ship
QR is printed on the packaging, attached to genuine inventory only.
Step 3 · Customer scan
Customer scans the QR to verify if the product is genuine or flagged as fake.
